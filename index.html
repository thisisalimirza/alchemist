Why These Books?

As a medical student running multiple ventures under Brask Group, I've learned that knowledge alone isn't enough. Wisdom—the kind that transforms how you see yourself and the world—is what moves you forward.

These aren't just books I've read. They're books that have shaped my philosophy, guided my decisions, and reminded me of my Personal Legend when the path got hard.

Each journey through these pages is designed to be immersive, screenshot-worthy, and shareable. Because wisdom should be beautiful. And transformational ideas deserve transformational experiences.
